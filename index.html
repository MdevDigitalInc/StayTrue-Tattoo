About:

Opened in May of 2009, we are located across from Giant Tiger on Talbot Street in St. Thomas. Eclectic decor, including our artists' own work, and the work of other local artists, adorns the entire shop. We are a family-friendly place; however you must be 18 to get tattooed, or 16 with a parent's signature.

Come Check Us Out Next Time You Are In Downtown ST.Thomas

If you are not ready for a tattoo yet, you can still purchase some sweet Stay True merchandise! We are currently home to three artists; Rich, Matt & Keight. Check out each of their portfolios to see whose style suits you best!
